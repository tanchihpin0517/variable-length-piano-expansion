The supplementary material contains two folders:

inpainted-music: Music inpainted by the different models. (i.e., Ours, ILM, FELIX).

controll-number-of-bars: Music inpainted by our model to have a different number of bars.

Each name of folders inside inpainted-music and controll-number-of-bars folder has a postfix indicating the range to be inpainted.
Midi files with "_prompt" in their name are the music with missing segment.
